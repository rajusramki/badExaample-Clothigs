Who We Are?

Welcome to Bad Example Clothing Studio, where we believe that breaking the rules is an art form. Born from a passion for bold designs and unapologetic self-expression, our studio challenges the status quo of fashion, giving you the freedom to be yourself—loud, proud, and fearless.
Bad Example Clothing Studio fuses street style and high fashion, crafting bold designs that celebrate rebellion, individuality, and creativity for non-conformists.
At Bad Example, we believe fashion isn’t just about looking good—it’s about making a statement. Our clothing is designed for those who don’t just walk through life but leave a mark wherever they go. Every stitch, every design is crafted with the idea that fashion should be bold, fearless, and a little rebellious.
